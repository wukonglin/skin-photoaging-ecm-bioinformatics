# Excluded materials

This is a **public, public-data** reproducibility package. To keep it generalized and to protect
unpublished work, the repository **intentionally excludes** the categories of material below. None of
them are present in the repository or its Git history.

- **Unpublished manuscript files** and any extracted manuscript text.
- **Private raw experimental inputs** generated internally (not yet published).
- **Internal notes and working memory** — meeting notes, session/chat logs, internal QA/process
  documents, and internal correspondence.
- **Any personal or collaboration discussion** — authorship, funding, career, or similar.
- **Secrets and machine metadata** — API keys, tokens, credentials, `.DS_Store`, caches, environments.

Only the following are included: analysis and figure-generation **code**, **public-dataset manifests
and citations**, small **public-data-derived processed tables and results**, and the **generated
public-data figures**.

> If unpublished experimental data are later published, they may be added with their own public
> accession and citation. Until then they are out of scope for this package.
